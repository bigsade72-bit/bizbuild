Frequently Asked Questions

What’s included in the Complete 7-Day Launch Kit?

You get our flagship bundle: a full 4-hour Meta ads course (19 chapters), over 1,000 email templates, Instagram & Pinterest ads training, workbooks, guides and proven copy-and-paste ad templates to launch your offer in just 7 days.

Do I need any prior experience to use the Launch Kit?

No prior ads experience is required. The kit walks you step-by-step from zero to launch with video tutorials, templates, and checklists designed for beginners and seasoned marketers alike.

How soon will I see results after I launch?

Many students start seeing measurable improvements in click-through and conversion metrics within 30–60 days, depending on your niche and budget. Our templates and best practices are optimized to drive ROI quickly.

What does the Done-For-You Ads Accelerator include?

A hands-on setup of one full campaign: you send your creatives, we remix them into high-converting ads, configure your pixel, targeting, and ad structure—and provide 7 days of launch support to ensure everything goes live smoothly.

What do I need to provide for the Done-For-You setup?

Simply supply your existing visuals or videos. We handle the rest: campaign architecture, audience setup, pixel installation and ad creative optimization so you can focus on your business.

What is the VIP: Conversion Kickoff package?

Our premium, one-time launch service for serious founders. You get a custom landing page, up to 5 ad creatives, and a live strategy session—no monthly commitment. Perfect for product launches and service-based businesses ready to scale.

Is the Conversion Kickoff a monthly subscription?

No. It’s a one-and-done package. You receive deliverables and a live strategy call. If you want ongoing ad management, we offer that separately as an add-on service.

What if I don’t get results?

We stand behind our programs with a 100% money-back guarantee. If you can show proof of implementing everything in our course and you don’t see an increase in traffic, we will refund your investment.

How do I join the live strategy session?

Click “Join Us Live (Free)” on the page to open our intake wizard. Complete the quick diagnostics form and you’ll be booked into a live session where we personalize your ad strategy.
